Why We Built This

In a country where every second counts but phone lines always seem to fail during a storm, we built this system to make sure no one is left waiting for help that never comes. Since the Philippines is the #1 most disaster-prone country in the world and gets hit by extreme risks like typhoons and floods every year, we realized that many families in far-off coastal or mountain areas are stuck using old-school calls that drop or get disconnected.

This system changes the game by using a smart platform that finds your exact location and sends an SOS alert even when you are completely offline. By using an automatic SMS fallback, your GPS coordinates can still reach rescuers even if the internet is totally down, ensuring your message always gets through. We also added AI to give quick safety tips and real-time weather updates so people can stay ahead of the danger. Basically, we built this to replace slow, confusing emergency calls with a reliable digital lifeline that actually works in the middle of a disaster, helping rescuers save lives faster across the Philippines.

Intelligent Subsystems

Comprehensive tools replacing outdated reporting methods with speed, accuracy, and automation.

Offline-First & SMS Fallback

Functions seamlessly without mobile data. Distress signals are cached on-device and automatically converted into structured SMS texts targeting the GSM network to guarantee delivery.

Precise Geomapping

Eliminate confusion with real-time GPS tracking. The system precisely pinpoints distress signals across all Antique barangays, heavily reducing responder search time.

Interactive Hazard Maps

Visualize flood-prone, landslide-prone, and storm-surge vulnerable areas. Helps both residents and provincial authorities understand risk zones dynamically.

PAGASA Integration

Get immediate early-warning alerts for incoming typhoons, heavy rainfall, and seismic activity based on real-time datastreams from national monitoring agencies.

AI Situation Classifier

A smart classification engine analyzes minimal user input to instantly route the alert to the correct responder type: Police, Fire, or Medical units.

Smart Evacuation Routing

The system constantly computes the safest, nearest evacuation center relative to your current location vs. active hazard boundaries.
