ARPS User Agreement

Welcome to the Active Response & Protection System (ARPS). By registering, you agree to these terms designed to keep everyone in the community safe and accountable.

  1. Honest Emergency Reports: All SOS alerts and incident reports must be truthful and accurate. Sending a false emergency alert is a criminal offense under Philippine law (RA 10639) and will result in immediate account suspension and referral to authorities.
  2. Identity Verification with Valid Government ID: You must upload a clear photo of your valid government-issued ID. When submitting your selfie for verification, you are required to hold your ID in your right hand, clearly visible alongside your face. This confirms you are the rightful owner of the ID presented. Impersonation or submission of falsified documents will be reported to law enforcement.
  3. Right-Hand ID Photo Requirement: Your selfie verification photo must show you holding your ID with your right hand raised to chest level. Photos that do not clearly show the ID held in the right hand will be rejected and you will be asked to re-submit.
  4. Data Privacy (RA 10173): Your personal information, location data, and uploaded documents are used solely for emergency response, community safety, and identity verification. Your data will never be sold or shared with third parties outside of emergency response operations.
  5. Location Access: ARPS requires access to your device location for SOS alerts and evacuation routing. Location data is transmitted only during active emergencies and is not stored continuously.
  6. Community Conduct: Treat all citizens, responders, and administrators on the platform with respect. Harassment, misuse of any feature, or deliberate system abuse is strictly prohibited.
  7. System Limitations: ARPS provides alerts based on official PAGASA and NDRRMC data. During actual emergencies, always follow instructions from your local government unit (LGU) and MDRRMO.

📸 Selfie Reminder: When taking your verification selfie, hold your valid ID clearly in your right hand at chest level, with both your face and the ID visible in the same photo. This is required for approval.

Your participation makes our community safer. Thank you for joining ARPS.
